  • Project Design

    April 10th, 2023

    TruthSeekr: HCI Project

    Developed the idea for, designed, wireframed and protyped the TruthSeekr application. TruthSeekr is a third-party run fact-checking website that allows users to input a link and receive a score between 1-100 that represents the accuracy of the article. The score is calculated by comparing the article to a database of fact-checked articles and sources. The score is then displayed to the user along with a list of fact-checked articles and sources that are similar to the article they inputted. The user can then decide whether or not to trust the article based on the score and the list of similar articles and sources.
